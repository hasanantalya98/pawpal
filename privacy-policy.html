Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the PawPal mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application") created by Hasan Altun (ByAltun) as a Free service.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Application is designed to prioritize user privacy. No personal data is collected or stored on external servers. All information provided by the user (pet details, logs, reminders, and photos) is stored locally on the user's device using secure local storage.

2. Permissions

The Application may request access to your device's gallery or camera solely for the purpose of allowing you to upload photos of your pets. These images are processed locally and are not shared with the developer or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. These may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (if advertisements are enabled)
  • Expo

4. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since all data is stored locally, the Service Provider has no access to the information provided by any user, including children.
